Respond to a request for information

Use this service to respond to a request for information RFI from an Authorised Officer. Requests are issued under Section 109BZA of the Social Security Administration Act 1992 (as amended by the Social Security Fraud Act 2001).

Before you start

You will need:

  • your reference number
  • name of your organisation
  • name of the autherised officer that made the request
  • the information we have asked for

You do not have to send all the information we have asked for at once. You can send more than one response to a request.
