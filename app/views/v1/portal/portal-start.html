Respond to a request for information

Use this service to respond to a request for information (RFI) from an Authorised Officer at the Department for Work and Pensions. Requests are issued under Section 108 B and C of the Social Security Administration Act (SSAA) 1992.

Before you start

This service will ask you to:

  • enter your reference number
  • confirm the name of your organisation
  • upload the information we have asked for

You do not have to send all the information we have asked for at once. You can send more than one response to a request.

MP4 files are not accepted. 30MB is the maximum file size for each file.
