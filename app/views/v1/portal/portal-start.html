Respond to a Request for Information

Use this service to respond to a Request for Information (RFI).

Authorised Officers at the Department for Work and Pensions send RFIs under Section 109 B and C of the Social Security Administration Act (SSAA) 1992.

Before you start

This service will ask you to:

  • enter your reference number
  • confirm the name of your organisation
  • upload the information requested by an Authorised Officer

MP4 files are not accepted. 30MB is the maximum file size for each file.
